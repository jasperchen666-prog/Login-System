Former Dunedin mayor announces ‘unexpected’ candidacy for council by-election

Former Dunedin Mayor Aaron Hawkins says he will contest the Dunedin City Council by-election, called to fill the vacancy left by the sudden passing of Jules Radich.

Radich, also a former mayor of Dunedin, died on January 4 following a heart attack.

Hawkins previously served for nine years on the Dunedin City Council from 2013 to 2022.

In the last three of those years, he was mayor of the city before being defeated by Radich in the 2022 mayoral election.
